# Don't Quote Me

A quoting assistant for Australian tradespeople that works like texting your best apprentice.

## Product Summary

"Don't Quote Me" is a **Service-as-Software** product that sells the finished outcome — a professional, priced quote — rather than another app tradies have to learn.

The tradie simply:
1. Opens WhatsApp or Telegram (the chat app they already live in)
2. Sends a voice note + photos + video from the job site (one burst, no waiting)
3. Receives a full draft quote within 30–60 seconds
4. Fine-tunes conversationally ("add downlights", "use premium taps")
5. Gets a branded PDF ready to forward to the customer

### Why This Product Exists

Quoting is the single biggest unpaid bottleneck in the trades. Median conversion is ~38% (62% wasted effort). Australian tradies lose 10–20+ hours/week on non-billable admin. Quotes that don’t convert sit untouched for a median 29 days. Master Builders Australia and HIA report acute labour shortages and cost pressures. The market is desperate.

### What's Different

| Existing Tools | The Problem | Don't Quote Me |
|---|---|---|
| Full job-management apps (Tradify, Buildxact) | Still require learning software + manual work | Zero app to learn |
| AI takeoff tools (Togal, STACK) | You still format, price, and finalise | Done-for-you outcome |
| Outsourced estimating services | 24–48 hrs + expensive | Minutes + cheap per-quote |
| Generic AI prompts | No memory, no pricing, no PDF | Full service with RAG memory |

### Moat

- **Private RAG memory** — every quote improves accuracy for that specific tradie
- **Workflow embedding** — lives in WhatsApp/Telegram where tradies already are
- **Real-time supplier APIs** — Bunnings partner pricing + stock
- **Human safety net** — optional human review for >$5k jobs
- **Data flywheel** — the more quotes, the smarter the agent becomes
