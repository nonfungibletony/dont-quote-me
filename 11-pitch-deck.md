- Site visits, measurements, photos, BOMs, price chasing, revisions → hours per quote
- Median conversion rate: ~38% (62% of quotes are pure time waste)
- Quotes that don't convert sit untouched for a median 29 days
- Australian tradies lose 10–20+ hours/week on non-billable admin like quoting

**Result:** Burnout, fewer jobs won, eroded margins, and inability to scale in a market desperate for more housing.
