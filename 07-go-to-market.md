# 07 — Go-to-Market Strategy

## Positioning Statement

**For** busy Australian tradespeople who waste hours on quoting admin
**Who** need fast, accurate, professional quotes without learning new software
**Don't Quote Me** is a Service-as-Software quoting assistant
**That** delivers finished, priced quotes via WhatsApp/Telegram in minutes
**Unlike** bloated job-management apps or slow outsourced estimators
**Our product** is the only chat-native, done-for-you quoting service that learns your business

## Core Messaging

### Hero Tagline
> Stop wasting hours on quotes that might never land.

### Supporting Messages
- No new app to learn.
- No complex dashboards.
- No more sitting in the ute doing admin after dark.
- Just open WhatsApp or Telegram, send voice notes + photos from the job, and get a complete priced quote back.

|---|---|---|

| You still build the BOM and chase prices | Someone that does it for you | Done-for-you outcome — first draft in under a minute |

## Target Segments (Phase by Phase)

### Phase 1: Beachhead (Months 1–3)
**Target:** Solo electricians and plumbers in Sydney metro
**Why:**
- Scope is repeatable and well-defined
- Photos + voice work well for these trades
- Sydney has high job density
- Electricians/plumbers have strong WhatsApp usage
- Clear BOM and pricing structure

### Phase 2: Expand (Months 4–6)
**Target:** Kitchen/bathroom renovators, carpenters
**Why:**
- Higher quote values = more revenue per quote
- More complex jobs test the human review tier
- Strong word-of-mouth potential in reno community

### Phase 3: Broaden (Months 7–12)
**Target:** Builders, painters, general handymen across AU
**Why:**
- Larger addressable market
- Proven model from earlier trades
- Ready for white-label enterprise offerings

## Marketing Channels

### Organic / Low-Cost
1. **Trade Facebook Groups**
   - Join "Australian Electricians", "Plumbers Australia", "Tradie Tips" etc.
   - Share value-first content: "How I cut my quoting time from 3 hours to 15 minutes"
   - No hard sell — build trust, then soft-pitch

2. **Reddit (r/AustralianTradies, r/electricians, r/Plumbing)**
   - AMA-style posts
   - Honest reviews from beta users
   - Address pain points directly

3. **LinkedIn**
   - Founder/team posts about trades industry challenges
   - Partner with trade influencers (small but engaged following)

4. **Word-of-Mouth / Referrals**
   - "Give 5, get 5" — both tradie and referee get 5 free quotes
   - Monthly "most quotes won" leaderboard (anonymised)

### Paid (Once Product-Market Fit Confirmed)
1. **Facebook/Instagram Ads**
   - Target: Men 25–55, interests: electrical work, plumbing, carpentry, Bunnings
   - Creative: Split-screen — left: tradie doing paperwork at 9 PM; right: same tradie finishing job at 5 PM
   - Hook: "Your last quote took 3 hours. Ours takes 3 minutes."

2. **Google Ads**
   - Keywords: "quoting software for electricians", "how to quote faster", "BOM generator"
   - Landing page: simple 3-step demo video

3. **Trade Magazines / Podcasts**
   - Sponsor "The Tradie Show" or similar AU podcasts
   - Print ads in trade magazines (old school but tradies still read them)

### Partnerships
1. **Bunnings**
   - Co-marketing: "Powered by Bunnings live pricing"
   - In-store QR codes in trade sections
   - Joint webinars for trade customers

2. **Trade Suppliers**
   - Mitre 10, Reece, Tradelink — similar API integrations
   - "Preferred quoting partner" status

3. **Trade Schools / Apprenticeships**
   - Free access for apprentices (seeds future users)
   - Partner with TAFE for "business skills" modules

